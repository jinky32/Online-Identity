Changelog

    

        
The framework includes a notifications API, which is the preferred way to utilise alert messages within Vantage applications. You can manually embed a notification message using the helpers defined below.

        
@HTML.Alert(AlertLevelOption.Standard, "Standard Alert", "A <em>standard alert</em> with <strong>emphasis markup</strong>")
@HTML.Alert(AlertLevelOption.Info, "Info Alert", "An info alert with no emphasis markup")
@HTML.Alert(AlertLevelOption.Success, null, "A success alert with no emphasis markup or title")
@HTML.Alert(AlertLevelOption.Warning, "Warning alert", "A sticky warning alert with <strong>emphasis markup</strong>", true)


    

	

	    
The standard alert is composed of a div.int-alert. Alerts with different background colours to designate different types of alerts can be achieved by adding additional classes to the div:

        
    
            
  • For a warning alert: .int-error
    • 
            
  • For a success alert: .int-success
    • 
            
  • For a info alert: .int-info
    • 
        

        
Make any alert "sticky" to the top of the page by adding .int-alertSticky to the outer div of any of the alert boxes i.e. div.int-alert.int-error.int-alertSticky
